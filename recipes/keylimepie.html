Key Lime Pie

A curiously tasty key lime pie recipe for everyone to enjoy.

Key lime pie is one of those deserts that must be tasted in order to get the appeal. Beloved by some while distrusted by others, this wacky dish is sure to turn heads at the office potluck!

Ingredients

  • 1 (9 inch) prepared graham cracker crust
  • 3 cups sweetened condensed milk
  • 1/2 cup sour cream or plain Greek yogurt
  • 3/4 cup key lime juice

Recipe Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350F
  2. In a medium bowl, combine condensed milk, sour cream, and lime juice
  3. Pour mixture into graham cracker crust
  4. Bake in oven 5 to 8 minutes, until tiny pinhole bubbles burst on the surface of the pie. DO NOT BROWN!
  5. Chill pie thoroughly before service
