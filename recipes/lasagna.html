Lasagna

Extremely delicious lasagna recipe!

Everyone loves a good old fashioned lasagna dish. Here is a recipe for a basic homestyle version.

Ingredients

  • Pasta Sauce
  • Garlic
  • Beef & Sausage
  • Mozzarella
  • Egg
  • Lasagna
  • Parsley
  • Onion
    • Tomato Paste
  • Parmesan Cheese

Recipe Steps:

  1. Boil Pasta: Cook in a large pot of salted water.
  2. Prepare Meat Sauce: Cook sausage and beef with onion and garlic. Drain well, add the pasta sauce & simmer it for a few minutes to thicken.
  3. Combine Cheese Mixture: Stir the cheese mixture together in a bowl.
  4. Layer & Bake: Layer the meat sauce and cheese mixturewith lasagna noodles and bake until browned and bubbly.
